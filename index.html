01 / about

Curious mind. Builder by trade.

I’m Juan, a software engineer and Engineering Manager based in Buenos Aires. I work across Java, Python (Django), C++, and JavaScript (Node, React, Angular, Vue), and I love turning messy problems into clean, shippable systems.

Right now I’m Engineering Manager at Hybrid Bee Technology, partnering with clients and building the custom tools that keep infrastructure projects moving. I’m also writing stuff and building different ventures.
